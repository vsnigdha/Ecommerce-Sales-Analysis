\# E-commerce Sales \& Customer Behavior Analysis



\## Project Overview



This project focuses on analyzing e-commerce sales and customer purchasing behavior using SQL, Excel, and Power BI. The objective was to identify revenue trends, customer retention patterns, product category performance, and geographic sales distribution to generate business insights and recommendations.



\---



\## Tools \& Technologies Used



\- Excel

\- MySQL

\- Power BI



\---



\## Data Cleaning \& Preparation



The project involved importing multiple relational datasets into MySQL, correcting inconsistent data types, handling missing values, validating joins between tables, and creating a master analytical dataset for reporting and visualization.



\---



\## Analysis Performed



\- Monthly revenue trend analysis

\- Order volume analysis

\- Product category performance analysis

\- Geographic sales analysis

\- Customer retention analysis

\- KPI analysis (Revenue, Orders, Customers, AOV)



\---



\## Key Insights



\- Revenue increased significantly between 2016 and 2018

\- São Paulo generated the highest revenue contribution

\- Revenue was highly concentrated within a few product categories

\- Customer retention rate was approximately 3%

\- Most customers were one-time buyers



\---



\## Business Recommendations



\- Improve customer retention strategies

\- Diversify product category dependence

\- Expand into lower-performing cities

\- Increase repeat customer engagement initiatives
